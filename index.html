About AK Fitness

AK Fitness, owned by Akash Kashid, is located in Kolhapur. The gym offers a variety of fitness programs, including personal training and group classes. Equipped with modern facilities and expert trainers, AK Fitness focuses on helping individuals achieve their fitness goals. It’s a community-oriented space promoting health and well-being for all.
